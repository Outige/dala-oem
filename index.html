FAQ

Common procurement questions.

Do you offer OEM and private label manufacturing?

Yes. Dala supports OEM, ODM, white label and private label manufacturing across selected art and creative material categories.

Can you supply for tenders?

Yes. Share the tender specification, required volumes, delivery timelines, packaging requirements and compliance needs in the quote request.

Can products be customized for different markets?

Products can be developed around market requirements, packaging formats, languages, pack sizes, positioning and target price points.

Do you support export buyers?

Yes. Dala works with export partners and international channels. Confirm your destination market and logistics requirements during quoting.

Are certifications available?

Certification availability depends on product type, formulation, market and packaging. Confirm the required standard when requesting a quote.

What information is needed for a quote?

Include product interest, buyer type, annual or order volume, packaging requirements, destination country, compliance needs and expected timeline.
